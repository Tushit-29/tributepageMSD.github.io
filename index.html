MS Dhoni




Mahendra Singh Dhoni also known as MS Dhoni or Captain cool or Thala is an Indian cricket legend, a right hand wicketkeeper batsman who was born on 7th July,1981 in Ranchi,Jharkhand. He was the former captain of Indian team in all three formats. He is the only Indian captain who won all three ICC trophies for India namely -> T-20 World Cup in 2007, ODI World Cup in 2011 and Champions Trophy,2013.

He has also captained for Chennai Superkings in IPL and won titles in 2010,2011 and 2018.

Awards he got

  • Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award in 2007

  • Padma Shri in 2009

  • Padma Bhushan in 2018

  • ICC ODI Player of the year in 2008 and 2009

Records made by him

Test Cricket

  • Under his captaincy,India topped the Test cricket rankings for the first time.
  • Dhoni is the first Indian wicket-keeper to complete 4,000 Test runs.
  • Dhoni has the most stumpings(38) by any wicket-keeper in a Test career.

ODI Cricket

  • He is third captain overall to win 100 games.
  • Dhoni's 183* against Sri Lanka in 2005 is the highest score by a wicket-keeper.
  • First player to pass 10,000 runs in ODI cricket with having a career average of over 50.
  • First Indian and fifth overall to hit 200 sixes.
  • Most not outs (82) .

T20I Cricket

  • Most wins in T20Is as captain (41).
  • Most consecutive T20I innings without a duck (84).
  • Most catches as wicket-keeper in T20Is (54).

A biographical sports film is also made on him named --> M.S Dhoni: The Untold Story. The film star Sushant Singh Rajput played the role of him.
